Step 1: Getting to know you!

The first step will begin with having a meeting to discuss your brand. What does your company set to accomplish? What are your target audiences? Who are your competitors? We will go through all of that and more to determine how your brand will look and stand out from your competitors.

Step 2: Brainstorming

After our first meeting we would have a good understanding of you and your company. Brainstorming and research will be taken to determine what style best suits you and your brand.

Step 3: Rough Logos

With this stage we will discuss a couple different directions your brand could go. You will be presented with 5 to 10 rough logos, all black and white. When the logo is decided we will begin refining and bringing your new brand to life.

Step 4: Semi Final

During this stage final refinements will be made for typography, layout, and color palettes.

Step 5: Final

After these five steps you will have a logo and brand that stands out and works for you. You will stand out from the rest and you will be recgonized by your customers.
