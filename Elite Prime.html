Elite Prime

Elite prime is a smoothie company concept created to test brand development skills. Elite Prime is targeted for those who are gluton free, and for those looking for a healthier beverage. The brand needed to stand out. I went with a playful approach. light green was a big factor in my process. Raspberries, and Blueberries were my second inspiration for color. Then I finished off with a bold font to send it home!
